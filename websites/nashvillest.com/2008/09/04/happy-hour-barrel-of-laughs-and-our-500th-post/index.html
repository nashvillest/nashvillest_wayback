Happy Hour: Barrel Of Laughs (And Our 500th Post!)

4 September 2008 | 5:54 pm
Author: Christy Frink

  • You may be among the few and the proud who have been around Nashville long enough to remember Adam Dread, former councilman/radio host/stand-up comedian who spent the better part of his term with his foot permanently lodged in his mouth. Well, he’s back on the stage and opening up for comedian Steve McGraw at Zanies tonight through Saturday. We don’t know about you, but we sort of love public officials gone wrong. [Pith In The Wind]
  • The English First drama continues, but is expected to come to an end today after a judge decides whether or not the measure will go on the ballot. At the rate it’s going, we’re guessing it’s far from over. [WSMV]
  • Rep. Marsha Blackburn and former senator Bill Frist will have their time in the RNC spotlight tonight when they open up for Sen. John McCain. [Tennessean]
  • If there are any old folks out there, you may be missing out on your chance to freeze your property taxes. We don’t exactly fit the bill since you have to be over 65 to qualify, but you may want to pass the info along to any seniors you know. [WSMV]
