Needed: Waterbed, Wink Wink [Gems From The Nashville Craigslist]

September 24th, 2008 · Comments

There are so many things wrong with this post that we don’t know where to start. Maybe… the mirrored canopy. No, must be the puppy trading. Or is it that the puppies are being traded for a waterbed? Without papers? Wow. Thanks to Jason B. for the tip! 

Really want a waterbed (Middle Tn)

We are wanting a King Size waterbed. It must come with a mattress, heater, and liner. And drawer pedstal. That is all a must. Now if it has padded rails and a mirror canopy that is all bonus. Please send a pic of what you have. I can and will trade a malshih 1/2 maltese 1/2 shihtzu puppy for bed. Right now you can have your choice of male or female. They do not come with papers. They are only a few days old right now. They will be ready Oct 29. Can email pics of puppies.
