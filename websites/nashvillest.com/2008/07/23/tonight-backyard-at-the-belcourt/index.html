Tonight: Backyard At The Belcourt

23 July 2008 | 3:22 pm
Author: Morgan Levy

Okay, so we forgot to write about this two weeks ago when we should have. If you’ll forgive us, we promise to do better next time. The good news is that you still have four chances at attending Backyard at the Belcourt, a new summer concert series in the Belcourt’s parking lot. 

In addition to tonight, the rest of the shows will be August 6, August 20 and September 3 (that’s every other Wednesday if you didn’t catch on), and feature a variety of local artists for the odd amount of $11 per ticket. Catch Will Hoge & Ricky Young tonight at 7pm (gates at 6pm), or check out the scheduled list of performers to find a night that suits your fancy.

Did anyone go to the first show on the 9th? And the better question – was the bar open?
