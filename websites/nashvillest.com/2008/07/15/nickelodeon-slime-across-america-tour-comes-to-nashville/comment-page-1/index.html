Nickelodeon Slime Across America Tour Comes To Nashville

15 July 2008 | 7:00 am
Author: Morgan Levy

What’s green, gooey and has 18 wheels? The Nickelodeon Slime Across America Tour, of course. The “Slimemobile” will be at Centennial Park this Saturday for some serious slime action. This is a blast from the past for us, and while it probably still won’t be as cool as Super Sloppy Double Dare, we bet it’ll be pretty impressive for the kids. The event runs from 12pm-5pm with a performance by Epic Records’ MTV-made boyband, Menudo, at 2pm.
