Happy Hour: Mudsliiiiiiiiiiiiide

December 22nd, 2008 · Comments

  • Think your Monday was bad? Ask the folks whose houses happened to be in the way of the TVA’s ash pond after the tension wall collapsed this morning in East Tennessee. [WKRN]
  • Congrats to the 32 Tennesseans who won the inauguration ticket lottery! We can’t say we’re not just a little bit jealous. [WKRN]
  • Story lead of the day, courtesy of WSMV: “Miley Cyrus will be spending her New Year’s Eve in some kid’s bedroom.” We sense some Christmas vacation-itis. [WSMV]
  • Rob Blagojevich, Nashvillian brother of Rod Blagojevich, has hired an defense lawyer to steel himself against any investigations into his campaign fund. [Tennessean]
  • Scientists at UT have used some newer technology to reconstruct the face of a guy they pulled out of Duck River 30 years ago. Ew? [WSMV]
