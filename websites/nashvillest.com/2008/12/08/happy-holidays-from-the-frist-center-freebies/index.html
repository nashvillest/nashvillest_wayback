Happy Holidays From The Frist Center [Freebies]

The Frist Center is offering free admission on Thursday and Friday evenings through the end of the year. From 5pm-9pm, just wander in and enjoy poking around for free! All purchases in the gift shop will be 15% off on these evenings as well. We’re suckers for free days at the Frist (as you may have noticed).

Also coming up at the Frist Center is their Holiday Concert with the Nashville Philharmonic Orchestra Flute Ensemble. Sunday, December 14 at 2pm (and you guessed it, it’s free).
