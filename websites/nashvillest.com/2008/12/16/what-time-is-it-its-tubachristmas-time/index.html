What Time Is It? It’s TUBACHRISTMAS Time!

December 16th, 2008 · Comments

Today is Nashville’s 20th Annual “MERRY TUBACHRISTMAS NASHVILLE” concert, in which tubaists from anywhere remotely near Nashville come gather to play tuba Christmas carols. Over 100 tubaists are expected to perform, which doesn’t hold a candle to New York’s 500 tubas, but we’ll take what we can get. TUBACHRISTMAS may only be 20 years old in Nashville, but has been around since 1974 in some locations.

The free performance is at 12:30pm today downtown, at First Baptist Church (7th & Broadway). So weasel out of your day jobs or take a lengthy lunch break and head downtown to sing carols with the tubas.
