Linens ‘N Things: One Person’s Bankruptcy Is Another’s Treasure

October 21st, 2008 · Comments

Dealmongers in Nashville, take note: Our local Linens ‘n Things have begun to liquidate their inventories. The Linens ‘n Things out at Hickory Hollow liquidated and closed a few months ago, but who goes to Hickory Hollow? Everything in the store is 10-30% off right now, but they’re just in their first week of liquidation. There are locations in Cool Springs, Rivergate and Murfreesboro. The liquidation sale is also available online, but you’ll probably spend what you saved in shipping costs. 

Keep an eye out for Circuit City to be following suit, with rumblings of at least 150 store closings in their future.
