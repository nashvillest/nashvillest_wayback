Remember Meet The Titans

October 3rd, 2008 · Comments

Anyone interested in meeting a handful of the Tennessee Titans can do so tonight at the Green Hills Hill Center, as part of a free event put on by the YWCA to raise awareness about domestic violence. They’ll be signing autographs in many of the Hill Center shops from 4pm - 6:30pm tonight. Each of the participating stores will also be donating a portion of the proceeds to the YWCA Weaver Domestic Violence Shelter. 

Participating players (and their wives): Ken and Sandi Amato, Rob Bironas, Keith Bulluck, Kerry and Brook Collins, Ryan and Stacy Fowler, Cortland Finnegan, Vincent Fuller, Leroy and Christina Harris, Daniel and Jennifer Loper, Donnie Nickey, Michael and Kat Roos, Josh Stamer, Paul and Kenyeih Williams, Kevin and Andi Long, Benji and Tracy Olson and Titans alumni Eddie and Taj George. 

Participating stores: (yn) wine, Hemline, Merrell, E. J. Sain Jewelry, Jigsaw London, Johnson + Murphy, The Cosmetic Market, H. Audrey, Jake’s House, Pei Wei, PoPo Cool Kid Stuff, Monkee’s, J. McLaughlin, Swoozie’s, Anthropologie

P.S. - A little bird told us that the Titans cheerleaders were going to be there too. Just sayin’.
