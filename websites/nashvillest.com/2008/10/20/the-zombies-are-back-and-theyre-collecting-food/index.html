The Zombies Are Back, And They’re Collecting Food

20 October 2008 | 7:00 am
Author: Morgan Levy

Remember the zombie invasion back in May? Well, if you missed it the first time around, you’re in luck. They’re coming back to town as part of World Zombie Day this coming Sunday, and they’re even collecting canned goods for Second Harvest Food Bank. Awww, what nice zombies they are. Anyone is welcome to join the quest to set the world record for Most Zombies Walking Around Various Cities In One Day, just show up in your best zombie garb at Riverfront Park at 3pm.
