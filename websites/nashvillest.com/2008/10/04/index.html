The Frist Is Free Today… Again

4 October 2008 | 1:52 pm
Author: Morgan Levy

Oh, and that other thing we were too busy to remind you of? The Frist Center is free today, courtesy of First Tennessee Bank. We always seem to miss those free days, so consider this a prompt to get over there before 9pm and browse around the several new exhibitions that have opened in the last few weeks. And maybe even walk down to the Schermerhorn to catch a bit of free symphony day. Then shuttle to the Celebration of Cultures! It’s a conveniently free day of fun.

Last-Minute Culture: Today’s Celebration Of Cultures

4 October 2008 | 12:59 pm
Author: Morgan Levy

This week was so busy that we ran out of time to post about today’s Celebration of Cultures in Centennial Park. Many of our friends have mentioned that it’s one of their favorite festival-type events around town, and we’re excited to attend for the first year. There will be performances, food, a world market, and cultural exhibitions all day. Due to our laziness, there are only a few hours left for you to get down there and poke around, but don’t let that stop you. We’re going to check out the free day at the symphony and then hop on a free shuttle over to the Celebration of Cultures… come join us!
