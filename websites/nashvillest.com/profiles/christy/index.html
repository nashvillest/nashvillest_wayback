Christy Frink

Christy is a journalism school dropout from Hampton Roads, Virginia who still can’t really get enough of the media. She’s a social worker now, but likes politics and bets you didn’t know those two things totally go together. She’s a geek at heart, but she also likes keeping up with baseball, watching bad TV, burning through her RSS feeds, and taking pictures (and looking at yours.)
