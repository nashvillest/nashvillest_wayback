This Weekend: Country Music Marathon [Road Closures]

22 April 2010 | 11:30 am
Author: Christy Frink

We can’t believe it’s already that time again, but tens of thousands of people will be descending upon the streets of Nashville on Saturday to run (or hoop, or joggle, or something) the Country Music Marathon. Though the weekend weather forecast is looking a bit sketch at the moment, rest assured your Nashvillest will be out there cheering you guys on. For those who are brave enough to try to drive anywhere during this time, we’ve included your list of road closures after the jump. Good luck, runners!

Freeway closure info:

  • I-65 North & South to Metro Center/Rosa Parks Blvd. – 6:15am-1:30pm
  • I-40 East & West to Broadway – 6:15am-9am
  • I-40 East& West to Demonbreun – 6:15am-9:30am

For more information, detour routes and maps and all that, check the official site.
