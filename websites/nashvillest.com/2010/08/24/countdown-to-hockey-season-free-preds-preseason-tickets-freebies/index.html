Countdown To Hockey Season: Free Preds Preseason Tickets [Freebies]

24 August 2010 | 10:00 am
Author: Morgan Levy

Can’t wait for hockey season to start back up? Well, you’re going to have to. But when that time finally rolls around, you’ll be armed and ready with two free preseason tickets from our hometown heroes, the Preds. No strings attached! Just fill out the form (it will only take about 30 seconds) and wait for your tickets to come in the mail.

While you’re waiting, get your Preds fix by hitting up the Nashville Predators Zoo Day this Sunday, August 29, with this buy one get one free admission coupon. All the fun of the Nashville Zoo with a Preds twist, including games, prizes and giant inflatable bouncy things. Preds players will be meeting and greeting in an autograph session from 2-3pm.

The Preds first preseason game is a home game on September 23 against the Carolina Hurricanes. Click HERE for a full list of their preseason home games, and start figuring out when you’re going to use your free tickets!
