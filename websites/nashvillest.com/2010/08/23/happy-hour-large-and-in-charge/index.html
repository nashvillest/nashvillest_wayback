Happy Hour: Large And In Charge

23 August 2010 | 6:00 pm
Author: Christy Frink
  • Welcome Juno and Rosie, the two newest additions to the Nashville Zoo! Both are female African elephants in their thirties and will be keeping the two existing elephants company. [Tennessean]
  • The TVA had to cut its power generation in half at a plant along the Tennessee River because they were actually heating up the river and putting the wildlife at risk. Consequently, they’ve already lost about $50 million. [WSMV]
  • As the college kids pour into town, Tennessee is working hard to increase the college graduation rates beyond the current 43%. [Tennessean]
  • A Franklin mom was busted for holding a “drinking party” at her house for 11 minors. [City Paper]
  • If you’re running around downtown this week, pay close attention to the new road closures that start this week around the Music City Center construction site. [City Paper]
