The one on Music Row has some serious problems:

1) There are no signs leading up to it. All of a sudden you're at a roundabout!
2) What are the streets leading off of it? They are not clearly marked as you approach, nor as you go around.
3) The roundabout is WIDE, but it's paved with brick, and there are no markings to make it clear whether it's a one-lane or two-lane roundabout.
4) There's a GIANT STATUE OF NAKED PEOPLE in the middle of it! Tennesseans are not really used to this roundabout thing. Please don't distract us with naked people.

When done right, roundabouts are amazing. You can drive all over the place without ever stopping at a light or stop sign. I'd love to see more of them in Nashville, but please do it right!
