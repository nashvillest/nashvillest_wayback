Morgan Levy

Morgan is a 20-something transplant Nashvillian from Hampton Roads, Virginia who works in entertainment marketing. She does a little bit of everything, doesn’t eat meat, and is more of a geek than meets the eye. When she’s not blogging for Nashvillest, Morgan pretends to be an old woman. Her hobbies include crocheting, knitting, spring cleaning, growing vegetables, fixing things (like toilets), using her electric drill, baking, and the DIY way of life. She probably reads more RSS feeds than you.
