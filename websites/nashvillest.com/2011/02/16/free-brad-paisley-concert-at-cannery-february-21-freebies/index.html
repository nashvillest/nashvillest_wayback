Free Brad Paisley Concert At Cannery, February 21 [Freebies]

16 February 2011 | 3:00 pm
Author: Morgan Levy

UPDATE Wednesday, 4:25pm: Well, that was fast. Due to overwhelming demand, RSVPs are no longer being accepted for this show. Congrats if you snagged tickets in time, and if you didn’t… Sorry! Faster on the draw next time.

Oh, the perks of living in Music City. We get everything from basement Mumford & Sons shows to free Brad Paisley concerts at Cannery Ballroom, the latter of which is happening this coming Monday, February 21. So if a free show from a country superstar tickles your fancy, you’ll want to head over thisaway to RSVP. It seems that RSVPs are mandatory but don’t guarantee admission, so you may also want to plan on standing in line for a few hours on Monday. The cheapest seat when Paisley’s H2O World Tour comes through the Bridgestone Arena late next week will set you back $50, which makes Monday’s free show sound very sweet indeed.

Brad Paisley WalMart Soundcheck at the Cannery Ballroom, Monday, February 21. Doors at 7, show at 8, RSVP required, 18+.
