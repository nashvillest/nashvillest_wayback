Things We Like: Nashville’s First Food Truck Food Court

February 15, 2011 10:45 AM 12 comments

Author:

Tweet

Got lunch plans today? You’re going to want to cancel them (or convince your date to take a stroll), because Nashville’s first Food Truck Food Court is happening from 12-4pm. Head over to West End & 23rd, where The Grilled Cheeserie, Pizza Buds and The Bang Candy Company will be parked for the afternoon to fill your belly with delightfully unhealthy comfort food and heavenly sweet treats.

The Grilled Cheeserie serves gourmet grilled cheese sandwiches, warm bowls of tomato soup and some of the most amazing pudding we’ve ever had the pleasure of tasting. Pizza Buds is Nashville’s resident pizza truck, and just thinking about their sweet-and-savory homemade pizza crust is making our mouths water. Top off your indulgent meal with an equally-indulgent cup of hot chocolate with homemade marshmallows from The Bang Candy Company’s Cocovan, and you’ll be in a glorious food coma for the rest of the day. Worth it? Definitely.

Nashville’s first Food Truck Food Court. Tuesday, February 15, 12-4pm, West End & 23rd. Bring cash!
