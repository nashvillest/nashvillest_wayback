Nashville Rollergirls Kick Off Another Derby Season At Municipal Auditorium This Saturday

20 April 2011 | 12:30 pm
Author: Morgan Levy

It’s derby time again! (No, not that derby, although it’s coming up too.) The Nashville Rollergirls are strapping on their skates for their first double-header of the 2011 home season this Saturday, and you don’t want to miss it. Our Rollergirls were on fire last season in their new home at Municipal Auditorium, scoring an undefeated home season and climbing to #3 in their division and #18 in the country!

Their opening bout is a double header against the #1 team in the South Central division, the Kansas City Roller Warriors this Saturday, April 23. The first bout kicks off at 5:30pm. Tickets are just $10 in advance, and you can them at Ticketmaster, at the door (bring cash to avoid an extra credit card fee) or directly from your favorite Nashville Rollergirl. Get your tickets, then your calendars for the rest of the season!

2011 Nashville Rollergirls Home Schedule

April 23rd: Double Header
5:30 – Kansas City Roller Warriors vs. Nashville Rollergirls Brawl Stars
7:00 – Kansas City Roller Warriors vs. Nashville Rollergirls All Stars

May 21st: Double Header
5:30 – Vette City Vixens (KY) vs. Nashville Rollergirls Brawl Stars
7:00 – Jet City Rollergirls (WA) vs. Nashville Rollergirls All Stars

June 18th: Double Header
5:30 – The Chicago Outfit vs. Nashville Rollergirls Brawl Stars
7:00 – The Chicago Outfit vs. Nashville Rollergirls All Stars

July 16th: Double Header
5:30 – Classic City Rollergirls (GA) vs. Nashville Rollergirls Brawl Stars
7:00 – Minnesota RollerGirls vs. Nashville Rollergirls All Stars

August 13: Double Header
5:30 – Carolina Rollergirls (NC) vs. Nashville Rollergirls Brawl Stars
7:00 – Carolina Rollergirls (NC) vs. Nashville Rollergirls All Stars

September 10th: Double Header
5:30 – Chattanooga Roller Girls vs. Nashville Rollergirls Brawl Stars
7:00 – Naptown Roller Girls (IN) vs. Nashville Rollergirls All Stars
