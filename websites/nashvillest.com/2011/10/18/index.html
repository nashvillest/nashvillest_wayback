Get To Know Your Local Booze At TPAC’s “The Art Of” Series

18 October 2011 | 10:00 am
Author: Morgan Levy

Booze and Nashville: Basically our two favorite things to write about. TPAC will kick off year two of their “The Art of” series with “The Art of Jack Daniel’s” on Thursday, October 20. Jack’s Master Taster Jeff Norman will walk participants through a tasting of four of their signature whiskeys and explain the history, use and flavor profiles of each.

The series will continue with “The Art of Yazoo Brewery” on February 23 and “The Art of Corsair Distillery” on April 5. Tickets to all three events can be purchased for $75 total, or individually for $30 apiece.

“The Art of Jack Daniel’s,” Thursday, October 20 at 6pm at TPAC. $30 or $75 for the series of three events.
