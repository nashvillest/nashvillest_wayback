Sorta Recession-y: Nashville Originals Restaurant Week Is Back

24 June 2009 | 4:03 pm
Author: Morgan Levy

Nashville Originals’ Restaurant Week is back for its second run, after a successful launch last September. Modeled after longstanding Restaurant Weeks in New York, DC and other cities, participating Originals will be offering prix fixe three-course meals at $20.09 or $30.09 a pop. Our only complaint about Restaurant Week is that it’s too short – How are we supposed to try 29 of Nashville’s best restaurants in just one week?

Reservations are encouraged, and most of the restaurants have a set menu for Restaurant Week diners. However, at both Germantown Cafe and Allium, diners will be allowed the freedom to create their own three courses from any salad, entree and dessert on the menu. Pay close attention, because prices and menus vary greatly from place to place. Some even include a meal for two and/or alcohol! Read on for the rest of the details. 

THE ACORN RESTAURANT: Three-course dinner for $30.09. Closed July 5. Special menu. Reservations requested. Menu

ALLIUM: Salad, entrée, dessert from the regular menu for $20.09. Closed July 4. Reservations recommended. Menu

CABANA: Prime Grill’In Menu, $19.99. Menu

CHA CHAH: Three-course dinner, special menu, $20.09. Reservations requested.  

CROSS CORNER BAR AND GRILL: Choice of special meals for two, including a bucket of beer, $20.09. Menu

FIDO: Two daily specials (most feature local and organic produce) and two specialty coffee drinks for $20.09. Closes 4 p.m. July 4. 

FLYTE WORLD DINING & WINE: $30.09, three-course dinner. Menu

GERMANTOWN CAFE: Salad, entrée, dessert from the regular menu for $20.09. Closed July 4. Reservations recommended. Menu

JACKSON’S: Special menu, Appetizer, entrée, dessert, $20.09. Menu

JIMMY KELLY’S: Special menu, three-course dinner for $20.09. Closed July 4, July 5. Menu

MAFIAOZA’S: Chef’s choice “Piccola Morsi.” $30.09. Menu

MAMBU: Three-course dinner $20.09. House wines $20.09 a bottle. Menu

MIDTOWN CAFE: Prime Grill’In menu $19.99. Menu

MIRROR: Three-course dinner, special menu, $25.09.

NOSHVILLE: Delicatessen Meat Sampler for two, $20.09. Green Hills, Midtown, Cool Springs locations. Menu

PM: Three-course dinner for $20.09. Special menu. 

PROVENCE BREAD & CAFE: $20.09, lunch for two: Any sandwich on the menu, plus an artisan side salad per person. Hillsboro Village, Downtown, Green Hills. 

RUMBA: Three-course dinner. Special menu. $30.09. Menu

THE STANDARD: Three-course dinner for $30.09, special menu. Reservations recommended – mention Nashville Originals. Closed July 4. Menu

SUNSET GRILL: Prime Grill’In menu, $19.99. Menu

tayst RESTAURANT AND WINE BAR: $30.09, three-course tasting: signature salad, local pasture chicken fricasse, and creme brullee. Reservations required. Possible surprise offerings. 

TIN ANGEL: $20.09. Appetizer, choice of one of three entrees, dessert. Accompanying wines at a special price. Reservations required. Not serving Restauarant Week menu July 5.

WILD IRIS: Three-course dinner, $20.09 and $30.09. Special menus. Menu

THE YELLOW PORCH: Three-course dinners. Special menus. $20.09 and $30.09. Menu

ZOLA: Three-course dinner for $30.09. Special menu. Reservations recommended. Closed July 4. Menu
