Nashville is set to host a Snuggie Pub Crawl. Music City will make history this
Saturday night by being the third city in the nation to host this cultural
phenomenon that is sweeping across the country. The Snuggie Pub Crawl begins
at 6 PM Saturday night at 3rd & Lindsley. After 9 PM the Snuggie Pub
Crawlers will start crawling to other nearby locations, with the Crawl Route
ending at Broadway Brewhouse at 317 Broadway in Nashville.
Snuggie Pub Crawlers must B.Y.O.S. (Bring Your Own Snuggies) and get ready
to party while staying warm and cozy.
