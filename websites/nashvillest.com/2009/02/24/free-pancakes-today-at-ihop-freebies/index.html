Free Pancakes Today At IHOP [Freebies]

February 24th, 2009 · Comments

The summer season of freebies is still a few months away, but IHOP is getting us through the hardest time by giving away free pancakes today from 7am-10pm. Of course, if you give a donation for your free pancakes, it all goes straight to the Children’s Miracle Network. Sure, IHOP is no Pancake Pantry, but it’s free food and raising money for a good cause, which we can always get on board with. There are six IHOP locations within 45 minutes of Nashville, so check it out and see if you’ve got one nearby. Happy National Pancake Day!
