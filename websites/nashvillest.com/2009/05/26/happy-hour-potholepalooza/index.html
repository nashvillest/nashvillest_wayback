Happy Hour: Potholepalooza!

May 26, 2009 6:15 PM 2 comments

Tweet

  • This morning’s promised traffic improvements for Green Hills have been announced: Hillsboro Road is getting some resurfacing and a center turn lane. It will also be spiffed up with improved crosswalks, sidewalks and bus stops. [TN.gov]
  • Our Memorial Day traffic fatalities were surprisingly low this year. While we don’t mean to make light of the seven deaths that did occur, we’re thankful to have that number down from 13 last year and 21 the year before. [WZTV]
  • The Tennessee House unanimously voted to support homeschooling efforts this afternoon… In Germany. Yes, the country. [Tennessean]
  • Meg White of The White Stripes got hitched over the holiday weekend in her ex-husband’s backyard in Nashville. Now that’s a party if we’ve ever heard of one. [Tennessean]
  • Three local fashion designers are in the running to be on the next season of Project Runway. If they can make the cut (30 finalists will be narrowed down to 15), we’ll be seeing them on the teevee! [Nashville Cream]
