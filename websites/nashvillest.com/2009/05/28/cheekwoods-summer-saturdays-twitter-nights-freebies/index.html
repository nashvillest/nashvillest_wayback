Cheekwood’s Summer Saturdays & Twitter Nights [Freebies]

28 May 2009 | 11:00 am
Author: Morgan Levy

Beginning this Saturday, May 30, Cheekwood Botanical Garden & Museum of Art will be having Free Summer Saturdays. Before you get too excited, know that the “free” only extends to children 17 and under… But still, a generous offer. Every Saturday through September 5, admission will be free for up to 6 children per family. 

As if that’s not enough, Cheekwood has proudly boarded the Twitter bandwagon, and is hosting Twitter Night from 5pm-8pm tonight. Similar to the Frist’s Twitter night a few weeks back, just mention Twitter and they’ll let you in for free, no strings attached.
