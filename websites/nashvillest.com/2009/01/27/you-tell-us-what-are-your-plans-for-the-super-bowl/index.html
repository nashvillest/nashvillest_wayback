You Tell Us: What Are Your Plans For The Super Bowl?

January 27, 2009 8:00 AM 2 comments

Author:

Tags:

We readily admit that our Super Bowl tradition involves sitting on the couch with some friends and a bowl of Doritos. Maybe some pizza if we’re feeling ambitious. And that’s nothing to be ashamed of, for there are few places better than the couch to watch a football game. But we wonder: Is there a bigger and better world of Super Bowl fun out there? Are we missing out?

What are your plans for the Super Bowl, and is there anything fun happening around town? Or is all the fun really in staying at home, lounging around with junk food and slinging profanities at the TV? Because if it is, we’ve got that covered.
