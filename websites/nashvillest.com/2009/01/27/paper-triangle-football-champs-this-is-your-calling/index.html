Paper Triangle Football Champs, This Is Your Calling…

Only the talented need apply: JACK-FM’s “Super Duper Paper Triangle Football Tournament… Bowl” is this weekend and only the best and the brightest of paper-triangle-football players need apply. Or actually, if you’re one of the first 128 people to register, you are guaranteed a spot. Otherwise, just show up at the Wild Horse Saloon at 11am on Saturday to register and wait your turn. The winner of the tournament gets $1000, which is some pretty serious cash for flicking a wad of paper across a table.
