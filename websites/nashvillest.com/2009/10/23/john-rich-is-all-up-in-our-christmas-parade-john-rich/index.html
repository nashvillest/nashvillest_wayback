John Rich Is All Up In Our Christmas Parade [John Rich]

23 October 2009 | 11:39 am
Author: Christy Frink

We realize there’s been a little too much John Rich in the headlines already this week, but we come bearing some more news: John Rich is going to be all up in our Nashville Gas Christmas Parade on December 4th. Fortunately we’ll still have our Shriner clowns and marching bands and gift-wrapped Piedmont Natural Gas trucks to cheer us up. We just hope he doesn’t break our bridge this time.
